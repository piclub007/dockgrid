FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What is DockGrid?

DockGrid is a free personal dashboard and bookmark manager that helps you organize everything online in one place. Save websites, notes, RSS feeds, to-do lists, and widgets on customizable pages. Available on Web and as a Desktop app for offline access. Created by PIReactive.

Is DockGrid really 100% free?

Yes. Completely free. No ads. No tracking. No hidden fees. We believe organization should be accessible to everyone. All core features are free forever.

How does the Desktop App work?

The DockGrid Desktop App runs natively on Windows, macOS, and Linux. It stores your data locally so you can work completely offline. When you reconnect to the internet, it syncs seamlessly with your web dashboard.

Can I use DockGrid as my browser homepage?

Absolutely! Set DockGrid as your browser's homepage or new tab page. We also provide browser extensions for Chrome, Firefox, and Edge.

Who created DockGrid?

DockGrid was created by PIReactive — a passionate developer building tools that make digital life better. Built with love and shipped from the future.

Is my data safe?

Your privacy is our priority. We don't sell data, don't track browsing, and don't serve ads. With the Desktop App, your data stays on your machine.
